Coconut Curry Soup with Dumplings


This Thai Coconut Curry Soup with Dumplings is the warm hug you need
when it's cold outside! From the ease of making it, to how delicious
this is, it's unbelievable! I am talking about a restaurant-style
Coconut Curry Broth with dumplings. My goodness, this is heaven in a bowl.
You have got to try it before you move on scrolling to your next dinner option!



~ Ingredients ~


  • 1 Tablespoon Avocado Oil
  • 1 Cup Onions, finely chopped
  • 4 Scallions, finely chopped
  • Â½ Cup Cremini Mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 1 Tablespoon Garlic, minced
  • 1 Tablespoon Red Thai Curry Paste
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 3 Cups Vegetable Broth
  • 1 Teaspoon Soy Sauce
  • 1 Cup Coconut Milk
  • 1 Teaspoon Sugar
  • 1 Bag Frozen Vegan Dumplings, about 12-15 dumplings

    • Garnishings

  • 2-3 Teaspoons Chili Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon Scallions (the green part of the scallions)

    • ~ Instructions ~

    1. In a heavy bottom pot, on a medium low flame, add oil.
    2. Once hot, add finely chooped onions, white part of finely chopped scallions and garlic.
    3. To this add salt. Cook until the onions soften and start to caramalize.
    4. Now add the Mushrooms. Cook them until they soften.
    5. Add the red Thai Curry Paste, sugar and soy sauce and saute.
    6. Next add in the vegetable broth, and bring it to simmer.
    7. Add the coconut milk, and mix. Bring it to a simmer.
    8. Now add 1 Bag of frozen Dumplings, it should have about 15 pieces!
    9. Cook this on a medium low flame for another 7 minutes. The dumplings should be thawed and ready!
    Serve hot! Garnish with chili oil, scallion greens, cilantro and crispy garlic.

    ~ Notes ~


    • To make it gluten-free, use gluten-free dumplings of your choice.
    • If you have leftovers, store it in an airtight container. Heat it up in the microwave to enjoy it the next day!
      I would keep this in the refrigerator no more than 2 days.
    • The recipe is super simple and straightforward.
    • You can add any veggies and protein of your choice to the recipe to make it healthier. The recipe is very forgiving!
